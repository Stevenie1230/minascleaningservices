What We Do

Our Cleaning Services

From pressure washing to on-set production support, we bring the right tools, team, and expertise to every job.

Trash Sweeps

Efficient removal of debris, trash, and unwanted materials from properties, production sets, event locations, and commercial spaces. Critical for keeping film & TV sets camera-ready and safe for cast and crew.

Pressure & Power Washing

Professional exterior cleaning for buildings, sidewalks, driveways, parking areas, walkways, and outdoor surfaces. Hot water pressure washing available to tackle oil, grease, tire marks, and stubborn buildup.

Window Cleaning

Careful, detailed window cleaning for homes, businesses, storefronts, offices, production locations, and event spaces. We leave every pane clear, spotless, and polished — improving curb appeal and first impressions.

Carpet & Upholstery Cleaning

Deep cleaning and sanitizing for carpets, rugs, furniture, and upholstered surfaces. Removes dirt, buildup, stains, and odors — leaving indoor spaces fresher and more comfortable.

On-Set & Emergency Clean-Up

Fast, professional cleaning support for TV and film productions. We work efficiently around crews before, during, and after production — and respond quickly when unexpected clean-up situations arise.

Restroom Attendant Services

Keeping facilities clean, stocked, and presentable throughout the day. Ideal for productions, events, commercial properties, and high-traffic locations where restroom quality directly impacts guest experience.

Commercial Cleaning

Customized cleaning solutions for offices, studios, storefronts, commercial buildings, and event spaces. One-time deep cleans, regular maintenance, or specialty services — tailored to your business needs.

Residential Cleaning

Deep cleaning, move-in/move-out cleaning, carpet care, window cleaning, pressure washing, and general home cleaning support. We help homeowners and renters keep their spaces clean, comfortable, and welcoming.
